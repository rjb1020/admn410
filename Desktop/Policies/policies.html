Financing


- We offer a lay away program to our customers who can not
afford to pay their entire bill at once

- Customers may pay 25% of their bill upfront, and continuously
pay $10 per/week until their total bill is paid off.

- Our lay away program is interest free.

Delivery


Three delivery methods are available

1. Standard 5-7 day shipping with a flat rate of $5
per order

2. Expedited 1-2 day shipping with a flat rate of $12
per order

3. Pick up your order at our store location in
23 Edgewood Road, Durham, NH

- Order will be ready as soon as possible (Free)

Return Policies


- Any food and/or perishable item can only be returned within
7 days of original purchase date.

- Toys and other animal supplies may be returned for a full
refund within 30.

- Returns between 30-60 days from original purchase date are
given store credit for amount of original purchase bill.

- Customers are responsible for shipping & handling fee if
returning item through delivery.
